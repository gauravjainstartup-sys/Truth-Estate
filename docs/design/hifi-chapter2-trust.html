📖 What these terms mean — tap any time; no jargon left unexplained ⌄
On-time record
Share of delivered projects handed over on or before the possession date the builder committed in its RERA filing.
Delivered
Project has received its occupation / completion certificate — legally ready to live in.
Ongoing
Under active construction and still within its RERA-registered timeline.
Lapsed RED FLAG
RERA registration expired without completion or a filed extension. Buyers of a lapsed project lose the RERA safety net — refunds, penalties and the regulator's protection get much harder to enforce.
Avg slippage
Average gap, in months, between the RERA-promised and the actual handover date across all delivered projects.
