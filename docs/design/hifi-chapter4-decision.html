Straight answers
Is DLF The Arbour actually a safe investment? ⌄
Mostly yes — an 88 Truth Score, sold out, and tracking ahead of its RERA schedule. The single thing to watch isn't the project, it's the developer: DLF has a Supreme-Court possession-delay loss and a CCI penalty history. See Chapter II · Legal →
What's the realistic possession date? ⌄
RERA commits to Mar 2030; our model forecasts Nov 2029 (4.7 months early) with a 28% chance of any delay. Insist the revised RERA date is written into your builder-buyer agreement.
Primary or resale — which is the smarter entry? ⌄
Often resale (~8–10% cheaper) — but mind the cash-flow trade-off: a construction-linked primary plan can deliver a higher XIRR. See Chapter III →
What's the all-in cost beyond the ₹4.76 Cr sticker? ⌄
Budget stamp duty & registration (~7%), GST where applicable, IFMS, parking and club charges — roughly 8–10% on top of base.
Can I buy as an NRI, and handle handover from abroad? ⌄
Yes — permitted under FEMA. We manage the paperwork, payments and remote handover so you never need to fly down mid-build.
