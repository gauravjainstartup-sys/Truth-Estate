⚑ Truth Estate's call
Don't commit capital yet. This is a hold, not a buy.

The location and the developer are genuinely good — but none of that matters while the title is contested and the RERA registration is lapsing. A great home on a clouded title is still a bad purchase. Wait for the flags to clear, or walk.
